IDUKKI

High Wavy Mountains

Idukki is a district in the Indian state of Kerala. It was constituted on 26 January 1972, by taking Peerumade, Udumbanchola, Devikulam taluks from Kottayam district and Thodupuzha taluk of Ernakulam district. Its division was previously headquartered at Kottayam city, but moved to Kuyilimala near Painavu and Cheruthoni in June 1976. Idukki district lies amid the Cardamom Hills of Western Ghats in Kerala. Though it is the second-largest district in the region in terms of area, it has the lowest population density among the districts of Kerala. There are only two municipalities in the district and the urban population is comparatively much lower than the rural population.



Munnar

Munnar is the most famous holiday destination in Kerala. Munnar is situated around 1600m above sea level and famous for its extensive stretch of tea plantations, high mountains and unspoiled valleys. Munnar is a perfect destination for wildlife and nature lovers. Munnar is blessed with a cool climate throughout the year.

Thekkady

Thekkady is the location of the Periyar National Park in Idukki district. Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is the main attraction of Thekkady.This place is famous for its moist deciduous forests and grass lands. We can see Elephants, Sambar, Tigers here.Thekkady is famous for its dense evergreen forests, picturesque plantations and hill towns.
Idukki Dam

Idukki arch dam, the first of its kind in India, is built between two huge hills Kuravanmala and Kurathimala across the river Periyar. This is the world's second and Asia's first arch dam. This dam was constructed along with two other dams at Cheruthoni and Kulamavu. Together, the three dams have created an artificial lake. Idukki Dam is 554 feet tall.
Vagamon

Vagamon is a hill station located in Idukki border. A perfect tourist place situated 1200 meters above the sea level. Spot surrounded by green meadows, the greenery of tea gardens,murmuring Pine forest,small waterfalls.Grass-covered hills, velvet lawns and the cool mountain air make Vagamon a perfect holiday retreat.
